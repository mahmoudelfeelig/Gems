# Synergies

Synergies trigger when multiple gems (or specific abilities) are cast within a short window (default 3 seconds). This works across **mutually trusted** allies and also for multi-gem players (Prism). Synergy effects are unique bonuses with their own cooldowns. Cooldowns and per-synergy overrides live in `synergies.entries` inside `config/gems/balance.json`.
